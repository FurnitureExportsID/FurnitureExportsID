About Us

The Origin of "Wood Craft Export" Brand

Founded in Semarang, Indonesia, began with a mission to bring the beauty and quality of Indonesian wood to the world. Inspired by the richness of our culture and abundant natural resources, we embarked on this journey with passion and commitment to create furniture that exudes warmth, durability, and high artistic value.

Our Vision

CV. Kalika Integraha envisions itself as a leading mahogany wood furniture company that not only delivers high-quality products but also fosters a well-integrated business ecosystem. We believe that true success is achieved by building strong relationships founded on trust and transparency. At CV. Kalika, honesty and integrity are core values that guide every aspect of our business. We are committed to maintaining the trust of our customers by providing sincere and responsible service. For us, relationships with customers go beyond transactions—they are long-term partnerships built on mutual support. Using premium mahogany wood as our main material, CV. Kalika is dedicated to crafting the finest furniture that combines beauty, strength, and durability. Our vision is to continuously raise the bar for quality and design, ensuring that our products not only meet but exceed customer expectations. Through this vision, CV. Kalika Integraha is determined to grow as a trusted furniture company, becoming the partner of choice in both local and international markets. We stand firmly on principles of honesty and quality, creating enduring value for every customer.
